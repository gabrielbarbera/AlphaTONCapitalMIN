Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about AlphaTON Capital Corp., our investment strategy, and the TON ecosystem.

General Questions

  • AlphaTON Capital Corp. (Nasdaq: ATON) is a publicly traded investment company focused on the Telegram Open Network (TON) ecosystem. We bridge institutional capital with the crypto ecosystem through regulated, transparent investment vehicles, providing institutional-grade access to TON blockchain's innovative DeFi protocols and infrastructure.

  • The Telegram Open Network (TON) is a decentralized blockchain platform originally developed by Telegram. It features high-speed transactions, low fees, and a scalable architecture. TON supports smart contracts, DeFi protocols, and various decentralized applications, making it an attractive ecosystem for institutional investment.

  • AlphaTON Capital Corp. trades on the Nasdaq under the ticker symbol "ATON". You can purchase shares through any licensed broker or investment platform. We recommend consulting with a financial advisor to determine if ATON fits your investment objectives and risk tolerance.

Investment Strategy

  • AlphaTON invests in various TON ecosystem projects including DeFi protocols, infrastructure providers, gaming applications, and innovative blockchain solutions. We focus on projects with strong fundamentals, experienced teams, and clear utility within the TON ecosystem. Our investment approach emphasizes due diligence, regulatory compliance, and long-term value creation.

  • Risk management is central to our investment approach. We employ diversified portfolios, thorough due diligence processes, and continuous monitoring of portfolio companies. Our compliance framework ensures adherence to regulatory requirements, and we maintain transparent reporting to investors. We also work closely with legal and regulatory experts to navigate the evolving crypto regulatory landscape.

  • Since our inception, AlphaTON has achieved strong performance across our investment portfolio. We've successfully invested in over 15 TON ecosystem projects, with several achieving significant growth and market recognition. Our Q4 2024 results showed 25% growth in assets under management and 18% increase in net revenue. Detailed performance metrics are available in our quarterly reports.

Regulatory & Compliance

  • Yes, AlphaTON Capital Corp. is a publicly traded company listed on Nasdaq and subject to SEC regulations. We maintain strict compliance with all applicable securities laws, including regular SEC filings, transparent financial reporting, and adherence to corporate governance standards. Our operations are designed to meet institutional-grade compliance requirements.

  • We maintain a comprehensive compliance framework that includes regular legal reviews, regulatory monitoring, and internal controls. Our compliance team works with external legal counsel to ensure adherence to evolving regulations. We also implement robust KYC/AML procedures and maintain detailed records of all investment activities for regulatory review.

  • Investing in crypto-related companies involves significant risks including regulatory uncertainty, market volatility, technology risks, and liquidity concerns. The crypto market is highly speculative and can experience extreme price fluctuations. Investors should carefully consider their risk tolerance and investment objectives before investing. Please review our SEC filings for detailed risk factors.

Financial Information

  • All of AlphaTON's financial reports, including quarterly earnings, annual reports, and SEC filings, are available on our Financials & Filings page. You can also access these documents through the SEC's EDGAR database using our CIK number. We also provide investor presentations and earnings call transcripts for additional context.

  • Currently, AlphaTON Capital Corp. does not pay regular dividends. As a growth-focused company investing in the rapidly evolving TON ecosystem, we reinvest our profits to maximize long-term shareholder value through strategic investments and portfolio expansion. Dividend policy will be reviewed periodically by our Board of Directors based on company performance and market conditions.

  • AlphaTON reports earnings quarterly, following the standard SEC reporting schedule. We typically release quarterly results within 45 days of quarter-end and hold earnings calls to discuss results with investors and analysts. All earnings announcements, reports, and call information are posted on our website and filed with the SEC.
