Corporate Governance

AlphaTON Capital Corp. is committed to the highest standards of corporate governance, transparency, and accountability in all our operations.

Governance Overview

Our Commitment

AlphaTON Capital Corp. maintains the highest standards of corporate governance to ensure transparency, accountability, and protection of shareholder interests. Our governance framework is designed to promote ethical business practices and long-term value creation.

  • Independent board oversight
  • Transparent financial reporting
  • Ethical business practices
  • Risk management protocols
  • Regulatory compliance

Governance Principles

Our corporate governance is built on fundamental principles that guide our decision-making and operations across all levels of the organization.

  • Board independence and diversity
  • Clear separation of roles and responsibilities
  • Effective risk oversight
  • Stakeholder engagement
  • Continuous improvement

Compliance & Risk Management

Regulatory Compliance

AlphaTON Capital Corp. maintains strict compliance with all applicable securities laws and regulations, including SEC requirements and Nasdaq listing standards.

  • SEC reporting compliance
  • Nasdaq listing requirements
  • Anti-money laundering (AML) procedures
  • Know Your Customer (KYC) protocols
  • Data protection and privacy

Risk Management Framework

Our comprehensive risk management framework identifies, assesses, and manages risks across all aspects of our business operations.

  • Investment risk assessment
  • Operational risk controls
  • Market risk monitoring
  • Regulatory risk management
  • Technology risk oversight
