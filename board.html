Board of Directors

AlphaTON Capital Corp.'s Board of Directors provides independent oversight and strategic guidance, ensuring the highest standards of corporate governance and shareholder value creation.

Board Overview

Board Composition

Our Board consists of seven directors, including five independent directors and two executive directors. This composition ensures independent oversight while maintaining deep operational knowledge.

  • 5 Independent Directors (71%)
  • 2 Executive Directors (29%)
  • Average tenure: 4.2 years
  • Diverse backgrounds and expertise
  • Regular board refreshment

Board Independence

The Board maintains strict independence standards, with independent directors comprising the majority of all board committees and serving as committee chairs.

  • Independent Audit Committee Chair
  • Independent Compensation Committee Chair
  • Independent Nominating Committee Chair
  • Regular executive sessions
  • Independent director evaluations
