AlphaTON Capital Corp. Reports Strong Q4 2024 Performance

January 15, 2025

AlphaTON Capital Corp. (Nasdaq: ATON) today announced its financial results for the fourth quarter ended December 31, 2024.

Key Highlights:

  • Assets under management increased 25% to $62.5 million
  • Net revenue of $3.2 million, up 18% from Q3 2024
  • Strategic investments in 5 new TON ecosystem projects
  • Strong performance across all investment categories

Financial Results:

The company reported net income of $1.8 million for the quarter, representing a 22% increase from the previous quarter. This growth was driven by increased assets under management and successful exits from early-stage TON investments.

Investment Activity:

During the quarter, AlphaTON Capital made strategic investments in several promising TON ecosystem projects, including DeFi protocols, infrastructure providers, and innovative applications leveraging the TON blockchain.

Strategic Partnership with Leading TON DeFi Protocol

January 10, 2025

AlphaTON Capital Corp. (Nasdaq: ATON) today announced a strategic partnership with Tonstakers, a leading decentralized finance (DeFi) protocol on the Telegram Open Network.

Partnership Details:

  • AlphaTON will provide strategic investment and advisory services
  • Tonstakers will integrate AlphaTON's institutional-grade compliance framework
  • Joint development of new DeFi products for institutional investors
  • Shared resources for ecosystem development and education

Strategic Benefits:

This partnership strengthens AlphaTON's position in the TON DeFi ecosystem while providing Tonstakers with access to institutional capital and regulatory expertise. The collaboration will accelerate the development of compliant DeFi products suitable for institutional investors.

AlphaTON Capital Corp. Completes $25 Million Series A Funding Round

December 20, 2024

AlphaTON Capital Corp. (Nasdaq: ATON) today announced the successful completion of a $25 million Series A funding round led by institutional investors including leading venture capital firms and family offices.

Funding Details:

  • Total funding: $25 million
  • Lead investors: Institutional VCs and family offices
  • Use of proceeds: TON ecosystem investments and compliance infrastructure
  • Valuation: $150 million pre-money

Use of Proceeds:

The funding will be used to expand AlphaTON's investment activities in the TON ecosystem, enhance regulatory compliance infrastructure, and build strategic partnerships with key ecosystem participants.

Investor Comments:

"We're excited to support AlphaTON Capital's mission to bridge institutional capital with the TON ecosystem. Their approach to regulatory compliance and institutional-grade investment management positions them well for growth."
